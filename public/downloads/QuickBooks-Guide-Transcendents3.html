QuickBooks Guide for Small Business

Transcendents3 – Texas & USA Book Keeper

This guide covers essential QuickBooks setup and best practices for small businesses in Texas and across the USA.

1. QuickBooks Online Setup

Choose the right plan for your business size. Set up your company profile with correct business name, address, and tax ID.

2. Chart of Accounts

Organize your accounts to match your business structure. Use standard categories for income, expenses, assets, and liabilities.

3. Invoicing & Expense Tracking

Create professional invoices and track expenses with proper categorization for tax-ready reports.

4. Tax-Ready Reports

Run Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, and other reports regularly. Ensure your books are clean for tax time.

5. Common Mistakes to Avoid

Don’t mix personal and business expenses. Reconcile accounts monthly. Keep supporting documents organized.
